Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is instrumentation tubing?
It is a precision tube used for fluid and gas transfer in measurement systems.

Difference between pipe and tube fittings?
Pipe fittings are threaded, while tube fittings provide tighter sealing using compression systems.

Best material?
Stainless steel is common, while Hastelloy & Inconel are used for corrosive environments.

How to select fittings?
Based on pressure, temperature, material compatibility, and thread standards.
